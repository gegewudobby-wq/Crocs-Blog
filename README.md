# Crocs-Blog
Crocs may not be everyone’s favourite shoe, but they are definitely hard to ignore.For our recent marketing analytics project, we studied Crocs collaborations and asked a simple question:If people keep making fun of Crocs online, why do some of them still resell above retail?We collected Reddit comments from r/crocs, r/Sneakers, and r/streetwear, and connected the discussion data with resale performance. We looked at sentiment, comment volume, and resale premium to understand whether “being hated” actually hurts a product.The answer was more interesting than we expected.Negative sentiment did not necessarily mean weak performance. In many cases, controversial products generated more discussion, and that discussion helped increase visibility.So maybe the real question is not just whether people like the product.It is whether people keep talking about it.
